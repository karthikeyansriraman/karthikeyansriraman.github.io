What is One Number?

One Number is a personal net worth tracker for iPhone, iPad, and Mac. It brings every asset and liability you own into a single, elegant screen — so you always know exactly where you stand financially.

No bank linking. No cloud accounts. No subscriptions. Your data stays on your device, synced privately through your own iCloud. It's the most private way to track your wealth.
